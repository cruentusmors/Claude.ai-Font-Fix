About Dyslexic-Friendly Fonts

  • OpenDyslexic: Specifically designed with weighted bottoms and unique character shapes to reduce letter confusion
  • Atkinson Hyperlegible: Created by the Braille Institute for enhanced legibility for people with low vision
  • Lexend: Research-backed font family proven to improve reading proficiency by up to 25%
  • Sylexiad Sans: Alternative dyslexia-friendly font with distinctive character shapes
  • Comic Sans MS: Often recommended for dyslexia due to its informal, non-uniform letter shapes

Note: Font effectiveness varies by individual. Try different options to find what works best for you. Some fonts may require separate installation on your system for optimal display.
